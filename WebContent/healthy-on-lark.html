Meet, Sharon

      Sharon has been a health coach for over 5 years with the sole purpose of empowering people to eat healthy and live better lives. Her restaurant features farm to table food with vegetarian, vegan, and gluten free options. Sharon became a vegetarian at the age of 15 after reading the book, "Diet for a Small Planet." She has continued this lifestyle for over 25 years, and traveled the world to learn exotic culinary styles.

      Sharon bought Little Buddha Tea Shop in June of 2015, and 2 weeks after she opened Healthy on Lark. She is so proud to be able to bring her health food to the Albany region.

 

"I'm very fortunate to have young people walk in and say 'thank you for what you do'"
