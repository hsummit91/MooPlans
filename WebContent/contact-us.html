Product Delivery

After we have successfully received your payment, your product information will be sent to the email address you provided. This may take up to 24 hours but usually happens within minutes. Please ensure first you have checked your spam folder. If you do not receive an email after this time period, please contact us.

Once you receive confirmation feel free to log in and start ordering right away. Your meals will be automatically credited to your account.

 

 

 

Refund Policy

Due to the nature of  Moo Plans products (paid in-bulk for a special deal), it is not possible to “return” the product therefore we DO NOT offer refund or exchange after purchase is made. If you are having any issue with the file, trouble setup, or have any questions, please submit a support ticket, we will do our best to resolve the problem.
