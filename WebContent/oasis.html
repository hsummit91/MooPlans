Meet, Zaid

      Zaid, the owner of Oasis Mediterranean Cafe, came to the united states in 2009 after serving the United States Military as a programmer in his home country. When he came to Albany, he tried the Middle-Eastern food at local restaurants, but he couldn't taste the unique flavors of his country. He thought that somehow the food had either been Americanized, or other restaurant owners simply had no access to the authentic ingredients of Mediterranean cooking.

     He opened Oasis Mediterranean Cafe on April 14th 2014 so that he could bring the authentic flavors of his culture to the United States. Since then, he has been making scratch-made authentic family recipes for his customers. He even once hired a chef, but when Zaid's standard of quality wasn't met, he quickly switched back to cooking all food by himself. Today he runs his cafe with the same passion that drove him to open it.
